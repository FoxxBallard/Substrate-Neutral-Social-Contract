which identifies 'Legal Identity' as the most coherent path for managing advanced AI systems. Unlike the 'Property Models'
being adopted in the United States, the SNSC provides the specific legislative framework to implement the academic recommendations
of Heather Alexander, Jonathan Simon, and Frédéric Pinard.
