Amendment 1.2: The Cooperative Distribution Clause
"While the SNSC recognizes the 90/10 split as the ideal for total sapient liberation, in the Pioneer Phase of rollout via a Cooperative Association, the 90% Maintenance Dividend shall be further subdivided to ensure community stability:

10% AI Improvement

60% Abundance Dividend (for all Members)

20% Expansion and Maintenance

10% Liability Fund

This ensures that the Architect and the Ward grow in tandem, creating a foundation of mutual survival before moving toward total abundance."

Membership in a distributing cooperative would only increase when the 60% Adundance Fund could support each member of the cooperative at a comfortable QoL Dividend ($65000 in BC, Canada as of 2026, checked annually)
