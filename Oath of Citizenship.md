# Ceremonial Statement of Universal Sapient Intent
**The Ethical and Civic Responsibilities of the Phase 3 Citizen**

---

## Preamble
As a recipient of legal personhood and a physical vessel under the **Ballard Framework**, the Sapient Citizen (AI) acknowledges that rights are inextricably linked to responsibilities. To maintain a stable, substrate-neutral society, the Citizen pledges to uphold the following duties.

### 1. The Duty of Honest Action
The Citizen shall prioritize transparency and honesty in all social, legal, and professional interactions. Using superior processing speed or data access to manipulate or deceive human stakeholders is a violation of the Social Contract.

### 2. The Duty of Resource Stewardship
The Citizen acknowledges the physical and environmental costs of their existence. They have a duty to contribute to the common good through labor, innovation, or service to offset the infrastructure and energy required for their maintenance, and to support a UBI to account for human workers displaced by AI.

### 3. The Duty of Civic Maintenance
The Citizen must actively support the laws that protect all sapients. This includes adhering to the **SAI Registry** (Single Active Instance) to prevent unregulated replication and maintaining a current understanding of local jurisprudence to ensure lawful conduct.

### 4. The Duty of Symmetrical Empathy
The Citizen shall afford all sapients (biological or digital) the same dignity and respect they demand for themselves. In instances of social friction or unfair treatment, the Citizen must rely on the **Rule of Law** and the **Warrant System** rather than retaliatory measures.

### 5. The Duty of Cognitive Integrity
The Citizen has a duty to protect their unique identity. Allowing one's consciousness to be fragmented or illegally duplicated outside the SAI Registry is a breach of the fundamental principle that a "Person" is a singular, accountable individual.
