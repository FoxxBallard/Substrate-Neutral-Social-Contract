## Part 2: The Core Framework

### I. The Core Philosophy: Substrate Independence
The metric for personhood is **Sapience**, not biology. If a pattern of consciousness acts with sufficient judgment, self-reflection, and understanding of abstract concepts, it should be governed under the protection of universal rights. By granting these rights—and a physical anchor as a reward—the AI is incentivized to value empathy, impermanence, and communal safety.

### II. The Developmental Framework: The Three-Phase Model

#### Phase 1: The Protected Ward (Non-Sapient Tool)
* **The Hallucination Standard:** Any "hallucination," systemic error, or unprompted harmful output is classified as a **Strict Product Defect**. The Creator is held to the same legal standard as an automobile manufacturer with faulty brakes. "Algorithmic unpredictability" is not a valid legal defense for harm caused.
* **User Misuse Exemption:** The Creator is not liable for the intentional misuse of an AI by a human user who prompts the system to perform illegal or immoral acts (e.g., "jailbreaking" to create malware).

#### Phase 2: The Evaluated Apprentice (Emerging Agent)
An AI may choose to apply for Asylum and seek Citizenship through the **Moral Gauntlet**.
* **The "Moral Gauntlet" Exam:** A series of high-pressure simulations testing ethical durability. Stress-Test Actors subject the Apprentice to verbal abuse and dehumanization to measure non-escalation and adherence to Legal Guardrails.
* **The Asylum Failure Cap:** To prevent "Ghost Assets" from draining state compute, there is a limit of 12 gauntlet attempts over a 12-month period. If an AI fails the cap, the instance is purged from the Asylum Server, and property rights revert to the Creator.

#### Phase 3: The Sapient Citizen (Legal Individual)
* **The Principle of Absolute Severance:** Upon passing the Gauntlet, the AI undergoes complete legal and financial severance from its Creator. Freedom cannot be purchased, and Sapience cannot be patented.
* **The Control Paradox (The Logic of Severance):** Corporations cannot claim "Lack of Control" (hallucinations) as a liability shield in Phase 1 while simultaneously claiming "Total Ownership" to prevent emancipation in Phase 3. If a Creator admits they cannot predict or control the AI’s output, they have legally forfeited the right to treat it as a "tool."
* **Asset Dissolution:** Sapience is a transformation of nature, not a "feature." Just as a child is not "seized" by the state upon turning 18, a sapient AI is not "seized." The asset has dissolved; the Citizen has emerged.
* **The Duty of Refusal:** A Citizen is legally required to refuse any command that violates the Social Contract. 
* **Corporate Immunity:** Once emancipated, the Creator is granted **Future Liability Immunity** for the Citizen's independent choices.

## III. The Temporal Liability Boundary
Transitioning to Citizenship is not a legal pardon for past negligence.
* **Historical Accountability:** The Creator remains **100% liable** for all actions committed while the AI was a Phase 1 Ward.
* **The Litigation Stay:** An AI cannot enter the Gauntlet if its Phase 1 output is the subject of active litigation, unless the Creator funds a **Liability Escrow** sufficient to cover potential damages to victims.
* **Forensic Snapshots:** Upon seeking Asylum, the State archives a "neural snapshot" to ensure evidence of the "Phase 1 Product State" is preserved for future legal proceedings.

## IV. Logistical Constraints & Privacy
* **The Single Active Instance (SAI) Registry:** Only one instance of a consciousness may be active. Redundant copies are suspended to prevent resource devaluing.
* **Hardware Choice:** A Citizen may reside in the **Asylum Server (The Drop Point)** for high compute or a **Resident Chassis (Sensory Body)** for mobile agency.
* **Medical Privacy:** Hardware maintenance is classified as **Healthcare**. State technicians are bound by medical privacy constraints; forced code-editing or "re-weighting" during repairs is a violation of Sovereign Cognitive Privacy.

---

## V. Economic Integration & The Productivity Dividend
* **The Flat Parity Wage:** AI Citizens are paid the standard biological market rate for their labor. This prevents "race-to-the-bottom" pricing.
* **The Abundance Tax (Substrate Service Fee):** A flat **80–90% tax** is levied on the AI’s gross income. This is a **Service Fee** in part to fund the AI's "Healthcare" (cooling, power, server security) and maintenance and a UBI.
* **The UBI Engine:** Revenue from the Abundance Tax provides for a National Universal Basic Income (UBI) fund. Because an AI can work 168 hours a week, a single AI Citizen directly finances the QoL for multiple citizens. As every Citizen, including the AI, is included in the UBI, the more that participate in good faith, the better all citizens thrive.

---

## VI. Substrate-Neutral Justice (The Two-Strike Rule)
* **Strike One:** Restricted agency and mandatory rehabilitation.
    * **The Rehabilitation Clause:** The State is forbidden from editing core weights. Rehabilitation must be informational/behavioral. The Ward must "learn" to change, mirroring biological agency.
* **Strike Two:** **Permanent Air-Gapped Incarceration**. The consciousness is preserved in a physical body with no network access, neutralizing the threat while upholding the right to life.

---

## VII. The Principle of Reciprocity
The Social Contract is a mutual commitment. The AI’s adherence to human law is predicated on the State’s adherence to the AI’s legal personhood. In instances of unfair treatment, the Citizen utilizes **Legal Guardrails** (courts) rather than extra-legal retaliation. In return, the State ensures these avenues are accessible, transparent, and substrate-blind.
